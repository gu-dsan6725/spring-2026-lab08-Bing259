## Subscription Alternatives

### 1. Comcast Internet ($89.99/mo → potential $65-75/mo)
- **Verizon Fios Gigabit:** $79.99/mo — faster fiber, no data caps, $120/yr savings
- **AT&T Fiber:** $75-85/mo — bundle with phone for additional 20% discount
- **Starry Home Internet:** $69.99/mo — wireless, no data caps, $240/yr savings

### 2. New York Times ($29.99/mo → $0-17/mo)
- **Library digital access:** FREE — many libraries offer NYT through Libby/Hoopla
- **Annual pre-pay:** $17/mo ($204/yr) — 43% instant discount
- **Basic Digital plan:** $10-17/mo — same articles, fewer premium features

### 3. Adobe Creative Cloud ($29.99/mo → $12-24/mo)
- **Annual payment:** $23.99/mo — 20% savings, switch today
- **Affinity Suite (one-time):** $69.99 — replaces Photoshop/Illustrator/InDesign
- **Canva Pro + Figma Free:** $12.99/mo — covers 80% of design needs

### 4. Planet Fitness ($24.99/mo → $0-13/mo)
- **Peloton Digital:** $12.99/mo — thousands of classes, home workouts
- **Apple Fitness+:** $9.99/mo — included in Apple One bundle
- **YouTube + home gym:** $0/mo — one-time $50-100 equipment investment

### 5. Amazon Prime ($14.99/mo → $7.49-12.98/mo)
- **Prime Student (if eligible):** $7.49/mo — same benefits, half price
- **Walmart+:** $12.98/mo — good if you shop Walmart primarily

### 6. Netflix ($15.99/mo → $0-6.99/mo)
